Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

CareFund is built to connect donors and verified charities with transparency. We only collect the information needed to run the platform, protect users, and process donations safely.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, phone number, and role.
  • Donation and payment records needed to confirm transactions.
  • Charity profile data submitted for review and approval.
  • Basic device and usage details to keep the platform stable and secure.

How we use the data

  • To create and manage donor and charity accounts.
  • To process donations, display live progress, and send updates.
  • To review charity applications and protect the community from misuse.
  • To improve user experience and keep the website reliable.

Sharing and protection

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We share data only with trusted services needed to operate the platform.
  • Payments are handled securely through the donation flow.
  • Charity records may be reviewed by administrators before approval.

Your choices

  • You can review and update your profile from your account page.
  • You can contact us for questions about your data or account activity.
  • We only send platform notifications for important actions such as registration, donation status, and review outcomes.
CareFund note: Our goal is simple — keep donor trust high and make charity data visible, accurate, and secure.
