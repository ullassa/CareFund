Legal & Usage

Terms of Service

These terms explain how CareFund works for donors, charities, and administrators. By using the platform, you agree to use it honestly, respectfully, and in a way that protects trust for everyone.

Account responsibilities

  • Provide accurate registration details and keep your profile updated.
  • Protect your login credentials and account activity.
  • Use the platform only for genuine donation and charity activities.

Donor usage

  • Review charity details before making a donation.
  • Understand that donations are made in support of charitable causes.
  • Follow the payment steps carefully to confirm successful contribution.

Charity usage

  • Submit only valid charity information and documents.
  • Keep fundraising descriptions clear, honest, and up to date.
  • Accept that CareFund may review, approve, or reject applications for safety and trust.

Payments and safety

  • Payments must be completed through the approved donation process.
  • Fraud, abuse, impersonation, or suspicious activity is not allowed.
  • CareFund may suspend access if misuse is detected.

Refunds and disputes

  • Donation refunds follow the payment provider guidelines.
  • Report transaction issues promptly with the donation ID.
  • Disputes may require additional verification to resolve safely.

Content and updates

  • Charity updates must be accurate and reflect current progress.
  • We may remove content that violates safety or trust standards.
  • Platform features can change as we improve security and reliability.

Platform rules

  • Respect other users, charities, and administrators.
  • Do not upload misleading content, false claims, or harmful material.
  • Use the site in a way that supports transparency and real community impact.
CareFund note: We may update these terms when needed to improve safety, trust, or platform operations.
