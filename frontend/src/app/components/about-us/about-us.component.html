Our Story

CareFund was built on a simple belief: direct connections create real change. We removed the layers of complexity that often stand between people who want to help and causes that need support.

Today, CareFund connects thousands of donors with verified charities across India. Every donation is transparent, every impact is real, and every rupee counts.
